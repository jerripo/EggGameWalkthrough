Hints

How do I find the code to talk to the magic girl?

Elise, the water pet, can advise you.


How do I talk to the water pet?

You need to find 3 apples, and then give it to her.


Where are all the apples?

Two are on the map (south of the knight, and top right corner), and the third is by completeing the archer's quest.

How do I get the key to the muzzle?


Answer the Witch's riddles. Then talk to her again to receive the key.


How do I get the stick for Sanura


Talk to the alligatorus (need key from witch). Keep following him up and down the screen. Give him a health potion to cure his cough. Keep talking to him until he gives you a stick.


Where is Sanura hiding?




I found Sanura 3 times. What now?


Talk to the magic girl again to summon Sanura.


How do I get a melon slice?


Talk to fox spirit, Toby, to get the melon. Bring it to Mai, the knight's pet, on the left to have her break it.


Do I have to give the thief the items?


No. You can finish the game without giving him the items.


What do I do with the girl?


You show her to the dragon in the south-west corner to show him that there are still pure-hearted humans in this town.
